Jake

Jake can morph into all sorts of fantastic shapes with his powers, but typically takes the form of an average sized yellow-orange bulldog.

  • Sex: Male
  • Species: Dog/Shape-shifter Hybrid
  • Occupation: Adventurer
  • Voiced by: John DiMaggio

Jake (full title: Jake the Dog), the deuteragonist of Adventure Time, is a dog/shape-shifter hybrid, referred to by others as a "magical dog", and Finn's constant companion, best friend, and brother.
